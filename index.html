Three Wet Frogs

A story about action, not just decision.

The Parable

Three frogs sit on a log. They all decide to jump in the water. So how many frogs are wet?

Answer: None.

Why? Because making a decision is not the same as taking action.

What It Means

Deciding is easy. Acting is what changes lives. Whether it’s starting a project, mending a relationship, or just jumping in—nothing happens until you move.

Don’t be the frog still sitting on the log.
